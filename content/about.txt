About JinniChirag Makeup Artist

JinniChirag Makeup Artist is a premium professional makeup brand founded and owned by Chirag Sharma, a celebrity makeup artist with over 9 years of experience.

Chirag Sharma specializes in bridal makeup, party makeup, engagement and pre-wedding makeup, and henna (mehendi) services. He is known for delivering luxurious, elegant, and customized makeup looks that enhance natural beauty while maintaining a flawless finish.

With a strong social media presence and a combined following of over 1.4 million+, Chirag Sharma has built a reputation for high-quality work in weddings and special events.

- Premium professional products
- Long-lasting and photo-ready finishes
- Professional and luxury service standards
