Frequently Asked Questions – JinniChirag Makeup Artist

Q: Do you provide bridal makeup?
A: Yes. Bridal makeup is one of the primary services offered by JinniChirag Makeup Artist. Bridal looks are fully customized based on the client’s outfit, facial features, skin tone, and the wedding occasion.

Q: What makeup services do you offer?
A: The services offered include:
- Bridal Makeup Services
- Party Makeup Services
- Engagement / Pre-Wedding Makeup
- Henna (Mehendi) Services

All services are subject to availability and booking confirmation.

Q: Who will provide the makeup service?
A: Depending on the selected package, makeup services may be provided directly by Chirag Sharma or by a senior artist from the team.

Q: How can I book a makeup service?
A: Makeup services can be booked through the website booking form. Users select a service and package, fill in their details, and submit a booking request.

Q: Is login or account creation required to book?
A: No. Users can submit a booking request without creating an account or logging in.

Q: How is booking confirmation done?
A: After submitting the booking form, an OTP is sent to the provided WhatsApp number. Once verified, the booking request is reviewed manually and confirmed based on availability.

Q: Do you travel for events?
A: Yes, travel is possible depending on location and schedule. Travel and accommodation charges are not included and must be arranged separately unless discussed in advance.

Q: Are travel and accommodation included in the service price?
A: No. Travel and accommodation are excluded from all service prices unless explicitly agreed upon during booking confirmation.

Q: Can I request a customized makeup look?
A: Yes. Clients can mention specific preferences or requirements in the booking form. All looks are customized within the scope of the selected service.

Q: How can I get exact pricing and availability?
A: Exact pricing and availability are confirmed after submitting a booking request through the website.
